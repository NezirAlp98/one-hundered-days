## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Authentication:** The process of verifying the identity of a user.
*   **Authorization:** The process of verifying what a specific user has access to.
*   **Passport.js:** A popular authentication middleware for Node.js that provides a flexible and modular framework.
*   **Strategy:** In Passport, a strategy is a self-contained module that provides a specific authentication mechanism (e.g., local, JWT, OAuth).
