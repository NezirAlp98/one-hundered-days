Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) is a common authorization pattern where permissions are associated with roles, and users are assigned to roles. Today, you will build a flexible RBAC system from scratch. You will create a custom decorator to declare the roles required for a route and a `RolesGuard` to enforce those rules. This will give you fine-grained control over what different types of users can do in your application.
